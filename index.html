About Us

Tarak Sir's Private Tutors offers tailored coaching for students in grades 8 to 12. Our comprehensive curriculum covers Mathematics, Science, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Accountancy, Statistics, Economics, Business Studies, Organization of Commerce, Applied Mathematics, SPCC, English, Grammar, Social Science, Gujarati, Hindi, and Sanskrit. With a focus on personalized attention and academic excellence, we help students build a strong foundation and achieve their educational goals.
